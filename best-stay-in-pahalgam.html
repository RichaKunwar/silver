deluxee Forest view

Imagine waking up to the stunning view of Kashmiris Valley and forests from your deluxee room and balcony, at Little Silver Hotel. Fresh mountain air and the sound of birds chirping will bring you serenity and tranquility.. You’ll feel like you’re one with nature surrounded by the beauty of Mother Earth.

Our deluxee rooms are spacious and affordable with the best comfort room, with all the amenities you need for a relaxing stay. A private balcony with a view of the valley, a king-sized bed, a work desk, and a TV are all included.

Our budget rooms are also intended to be comfortable. Every room features a sitting area with handcrafted wooden chairs and a teapot. The bed is set with a floral bed cover and is intended to give you a good night's sleep.

We also know that tea enthusiasts would appreciate our extras. Every room has a tea kettle and two tea sachets. You can have a hot cup of tea or coffee in your room or on our veranda, which is surrounded by nature with a mountain view.
